Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, etc.)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Procedure

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and cook for about 3-5 minutes, or until they are tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and stir everything together, breaking up any clumps of rice.
  6. Pour the soy sauce over the rice and mix well, ensuring that the soy sauce is evenly distributed.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is heated through.
  9. Remove the pan from heat and stir in the chopped green onions.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade fried rice!
